Company Overview

TM Squared Consulting and Services was founded in 2023 and officially registered in January 2025. The company is built on a foundation of Strategic Security Intelligence, offering Security Advisory Services at both macro and micro levels. TM Squared specializes in developing tailored solutions that address personnel vetting, information security, cybersecurity, governance, and logistics. The leadership team is professionally recognized through memberships with ACFE (South Africa and International), IRMSA, and PSIRA. These affiliations ensure compliance with both local and international industry standards.
